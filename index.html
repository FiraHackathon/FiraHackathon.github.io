Introduction

This hackathon is organized by RobAgri and his members Opal-RT in collaboration with 4D-Virtualiz and INRAE with the roboterrium TIRREX in the framework of the big challenge of the agricultural robotics.

A hackathon with:
  • Three topics to improve safety in robotics
  • Two stages:
    • The first stage of qualification with a light simulator using ROS2 and Gazebo from 4 December to 15 January in remote -> the two best teams for each subject will qualify
    • The second final stage at FIRA from 6 to 8 February in Toulouse with simulators from Opal-RT and 4D-Virtualiz interfaced with ROS2.
Conditions for taking part:
  • At least one member of the team must be able to travel to Toulouse
  • Have a team of at least 2 and no more than 6 members
  • Registration link
